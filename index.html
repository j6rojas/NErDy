About Michael Morgan

Michael Morgan, the heart and soul of NErDy at Bravo, has dedicated over three decades to creating a culture of academic excellence and inclusivity. Starting with a dream inspired by his time at the Institute for Chemical Education, he transformed Bravo into a hub for science enthusiasts. From organizing chemistry outreach programs to leading teams in Science Bowl, Ocean Bowl, and various Olympiads, Michael has built a legacy of making science accessible and fun for all students. His classroom is a haven for students, fostering a sense of belonging and purpose. Under his guidance, NErDy has produced numerous semifinalists in Chemistry, Biology, Physics, and Math Olympiads, with many students going on to prestigious careers in academia and beyond.

About Christopher Navarrete

Christopher Navarrete, a first-generation Mexican-American educator, is the Honors Chemistry and Honors Physics teacher at Bravo. With a B.S. in Chemistry/Biochemistry from UC San Diego and an M.Ed. in Curriculum and Instruction from CSU Dominguez Hills, he combines his passion for science with a commitment to education. Christopher's teaching journey includes leading science programs at "The U" in Watts, CA, and Stephen White Middle School, where he connected science to students' communities and other disciplines. At Bravo, he inspires students to explore the world of science and develop critical thinking skills for success beyond the classroom.
